Linear Regression - Global Population and Global GDP

Using data from Global_Population.csv & Global_GDP.csv. Perform linear regression where the independent variable is the mean population of each country and the dependent variable is the mean per capita GDP.

Because the mean values of GDP and Population are so large, I have used the StandardScaler normalization technique from SKLearn to scale the values.
StandardScaler normalizes features by scaling them to have a mean of 0 and a standard deviation of 1, ensuring all features are on the scale and prevents values from dominating due to large magnitudes (GeeksforGeeks, 2024). It preserves the shape and relative relationships of the features distributions by only adjusting their scale and position (GeeksforGeeks, 2024).
