Reflection

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is crucial for data science and machine learning. It focuses on understanding the data before making any assumptions. It's more than just gaining a deeper understanding of the data, it's the bridge between raw data and actionable insights, enabling machine learning projects to be more efficient and accurate ensuring the use of high-quality data for predictions (markovML, 2024). For me it is about sparking innovative ideas for problem-solving and identifying opportunities even without a specific problem to address.
