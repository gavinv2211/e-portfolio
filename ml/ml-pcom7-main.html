Machine Learning

Introduction

This module introduces machine learning. A field of artificial intelligence that focuses on building systems that can learn from and make decisions or predictions based on data, without being explicitly programmed.

Throughout the course, we encountered numerous challenges and engaged in a variety of practical exercises and tasks.

This hands-on course provided valuable learning experiences through independent research, teamwork, and the development of multiple models, including supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms as well as artificial neural networks.

Machine learning is a vast field, and i recognize that there is still much to learn. However, this course gave me a strong foundation and a practical understanding of key concepts.

Going beyond theoretical knowledge, we explored how artificial neural networks function, discussed the legal and ethical implications of machine learning, and exmained its future applications.
