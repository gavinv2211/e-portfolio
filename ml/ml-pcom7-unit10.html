Discussion 2 - Legal and Ethical views on ANN Applications

This discussion continues from the discussion in Unit 8 "The risks and benefits of the use of AI writers at different level, from administrative work to creative writing". Summary and peer resonses.

While the use of AI writers does offer major benefits, they also carry notable risks. Martyna Antas points out an important concern regarding uncritical acceptance of AI-generated content which can lead to the spread of misinformation if users don't assess the information critically. The article by (Monteith et al., 2023) addresses the growing use of generative AI highlight both its benefits and risks. The spread of misinformation from generative AI is a key concern particularly in mental illnesses.

James Adams expanded by mentioning that organizations may need to adapt their training and development strategies due to technological advancements, also highlighting concerns that junior employees might miss out on traditional learning opportunities due to a reduction in roles, while senior employees must continuously update their skills to remain competitive. James referred to the article by (Boritz et al., 2023) which represents an alternative perspective - if technology is properly leveraged, it can enhance skills, experience and cognitive abilities rather than diminishing them.

In summary, generative AI has both advantages and disadvantages. It is crucial to recognize that generative AI is not perfect, as it can produce misinformation and potentially negatively impact our cognitive functions. However, when used correctly and with an awareness of its limitations, it can be beneficial too.
