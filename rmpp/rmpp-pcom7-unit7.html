Before reaching any conclusions or assigning blame, it is essential to take a step back and gather all the relevant information. For instance, was the review requested by the cereal manufacturer, a regulatory body, or an independent third party? Understanding the 'who' and 'why' behind the project is critical for making an informed and ethical recommendation.

In situations like these, jumping to conclusions without the full picture can lead to poor decisions. By ensuring we have all the facts, we put ourselves in a better position to evaluate Abi’s situation fairly and to suggest responsible, transparent actions moving forward.
