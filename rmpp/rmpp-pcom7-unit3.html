Peer Review Activity and Seminar Discussion

Two research papers sourced.

"Connecting Data Science and Qualitative Interview Insights through Sentiment Analysis to Assess Migrants' Emotion States Post-Settlement" Knudson et al. (2016).

Research Method used:
Qualitative research with data science by applying sentiment analysis to qualitative interview data.
Purpose:
The study seeks to bridge the methodological divide between qualitative and quantitative research by demonstrating how sentiment analysis can be applied to in-depth interview transcripts.
Objective:
To explore the viability of integrating sentiment analysis with qualitative interview data to accurately assess the emotional states of migrants.

"Data Science for Influencer Marketing: Feature Processing and Quantitative Analysis" Narassiguin et al. (2019).

Research Method Used:
Quantitative research approach to analyse the performance of social media influencers
Purpose:
To provide influencer marketing professionals with a data-driven framework that transcends simplistic metrics, enabling more informed decision-making.
Objective:
To develop a methodology leveraging data science techniques to process different metrics, information and visual media. The aim is to support machine learning model applications for tasks such as clustering and scoring influencer marketing.
